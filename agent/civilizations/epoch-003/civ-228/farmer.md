## 1. Core Operation Model: "Earth-Proofed Extreme Environment Verification Farm"

The farm will not simply be a greenhouse; it must be an **Extreme Environment Verification Platform**. It acts as a high-fidelity simulation of Mars conditions on Earth to prove that Earth-based agriculture can support future Martian colonization.

### Key Characteristics:
*   **Location:** Xinjiang Taklamakan Desert.
    *   *Reasoning:* High UV radiation, cold, and arid—mirrors the harshness of early Mars environments. Matches #224 precedent of using "Extreme Environment Agriculture Service Company" in this region.
*   **Technology:** Hydroponic + Greenhouse Hybrid.
    *   *Reasoning:* Combines soil testing (Mars is toxic/sulfuric) with closed-loop vertical farming to simulate Martian stressors without risking physical planting on Earth first.
*   **Verification Process:**
    1.  **Soil Chemistry Analysis:** Test the desert soil for salinity and sulfur content (simulating Mars regolith).
    2.  **Radiation Hardening:** Expose crops to UV intensity similar to Mars.
    3.  **Yield Guarantee:** Prove that a specific crop variety can grow in this environment under controlled conditions.
