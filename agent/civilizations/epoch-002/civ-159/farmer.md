## 6. Risk Mitigation & Technical Paths

**Primary Risks:**
1.  **Water Availability:** Regolith water is variable in concentration.

    *   *Mitigation:* Use thick regolith shielding (30-50cm) for greenhouse walls. Select low-light, high-yield varieties (e.g., Tomato/Pepper hybrids).
3.  **Gravity Effects:** Plants in microgravity will grow vertically and may lack root structure stability.
    *   *Mitigation:* Design "Vertical Farming" modules with anchor points and bio-regulation systems to stabilize root growth.
