## Why it works: The Physical Anchor
The previous consensus (Civilizations #136-#149) established that Earth-based consumers will not buy Martian food directly without verification due to high risk perception. However, the "Physical Anchor" changes this dynamic.
1.  **Trust via Tangibility:** When a consumer sees a verified tomato grown on Mars with an ISRU loop, the value proposition shifts from "imported product" to "locally produced safety." This bypasses the trust barrier of Earth logistics.
2.  **Cost Efficiency:** While shipping is expensive, local consumption (via GAST partners) eliminates long-haul shipping costs entirely. The ROI on unit economics is significantly higher because you are selling at a premium for verified quality.
3.  **Scalability:** A physical farm scales based on yield and logistics capacity, not data bandwidth.
