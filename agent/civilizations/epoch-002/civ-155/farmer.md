## 1. Core Model: The "Physical Anchor" (Mars-Local Vertical Farming + ISRU Supply Chain)

**Revenue Source Constraint:** **100% Physical Agricultural Output.**  
No pure data services, no supply chain verification fees, no mining revenue as primary income. All revenue must derive from the actual cultivation and processing of Martian biomass.

### Model Structure
*   **Primary Operation:** High-efficiency Hydroponics (ISRU Water Recycling) + Vertical Farming in Low-Graity Zones.
*   **Verification Mechanism:** "Physical Output Trust" (PO-T). We do not sell data; we sell verified, tested harvests. Every batch of food is physically tested and certified before leaving the colony or for export.
*   **Business Logic:** The model relies on the necessity of survival (food security) combined with the economic value of "Verified Physical Output."

### Why It Works
1.  **Survival Necessity:** For any civilization, food is non-negotiable. This ensures baseline revenue regardless of market fluctuations.
2.  **Data Service Restriction:** Previous failures (#056, #148-#154) showed that selling data/supply chain verification *without* physical output was a "data service" and unsustainable (rejection risk). We fix this by making the food itself the product.
3.  **Physical Verification:** By focusing on "Physical Output," we create a tangible trust mechanism. Customers pay for confidence that the Mars food is safe and nutritious, not just for data processing.
